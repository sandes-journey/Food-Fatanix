Restaurant Reviews

One of the biggest challenges while exploring is deciding where to eat. Sometimes, the fun is in stumbling upon a hidden gem, but other times, you might end up in a tourist trap. When is a picture menu a good sign, and when is it a red flag? I always research before visiting a new city—having a few solid options makes all the difference. Stay hungry and explore!

Grimaldi's Cucina Sushi Bar

A fresh, contemporary take on Italian food that still maintains all the comforts we all love about Italy's cuisine.

Location: 101 Isaiah Ntshangase Road Stamford Hill, Durban 4001 South Africa

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Cargo Hold Restaurant

Enjoy a Culinary Experience onboard the Phantom Ship! Dine next to a tank of sharks, sip on the finest wine and so much more!

Location: Bell Street - Ushaka Marine World, Durban South Africa

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

FYN

At FYN, South Africa’s wild freedom is tempered by the rigors of contemporary cuisine to create a restaurant at the edge.

Location: 5th Floor Speakers Corner, 37 Parliament St Cape Town City Centre Cape Town, South Africa

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐

Sugerlicious

Ice-cream macarons, where you can choose from a range of different ice-creams which come sandwiched between two delicate French macarons.

Location: Shop 2 Palm Boulevard, Umhlanga Rocks, KwaZulu-Natal

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Gondola Rides and Ristorante Quadri

Dining at Ristorante Quadri while overlooking Pizza San Marco is the ultimate luxury.

Location: Venice, Italy

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

9th Avenue Waterside

Enjoying delicious food by the ocean, with city views and a sea breeze—pure bliss!

Location: South Africa, Durban
